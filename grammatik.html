Der Feldweg · The Farm Lane

The road to C2 runs straight and paved, and everyone on it is in a hurry. But there is a gap in the hedge past the last milestone, and beyond it a rutted lane winds off between the fields.

Six farms sit along it. No towers, no crowns — a goose pasture, a ploughed acre, a garden, a pond, a mill and a barn. The girls who work them have never been to court, but between them they hold every rule of German grammar the way you hold a tool you use daily.

They will not hand it over on the first visit. Come back often enough, and the walk to the door gets shorter.
